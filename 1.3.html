How Money is Made - Interactive Factory

Click through the steps to learn how money is made!

Step 1: Collecting Raw Materials

To create money, we need raw materials. Coins are made from metals like copper, nickel, and zinc, while banknotes are printed on a special blend of cotton and linen.

Step 2: Minting Coins

Once the raw metal is prepared, it is melted and rolled into thin sheets. These sheets are then cut into small circular blanks, which are later stamped with designs.

Step 3: Printing Notes

Banknotes are created using high-security printing presses. They include security features like watermarks and holograms.

Step 4: Quality Check

Every coin and banknote undergoes strict quality control checks to prevent defects or errors.

Step 5: Distribution

After quality checks, money is sent to banks and ATMs for public use.
