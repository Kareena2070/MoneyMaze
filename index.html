📜 The Story of MoneyMaze MoneyMaze started with a simple idea: Make financial education fun and interactive for kids! 🌟

💡 Our Mission: To help children (6-18 years) understand money through games, quizzes, and activities that grow with them.

🔹 How It Started: We noticed that many kids and teens struggle with basic financial concepts. We wanted to change that!

🔹 The MoneyMaze Experience:


✅ 10 Interactive Levels – From guessing coins to learning about digital payments.
✅ Step-by-Step Learning – Fun quizzes, drag-and-drop challenges, and real-life scenarios.
✅ Designed for All Ages – Tailored learning for young kids (6-12) and teens (13-18).

🔹 Why Choose MoneyMaze?
🎮 Gamified Learning – No boring lectures, just engaging challenges!
🏆 Progressive Difficulty – Kids start with simple topics and advance to complex ones.
💡 Real-Life Skills – Understanding saving, earning, investing, and smart spending.
